Disease Type : Bacterial Leaf Blight

Symptoms:-



Bacterial spot of pepper is caused by bacteria belonging to several species in the genus Xanthomonas.

Symptoms appear as water-soaked spots that change from green to dark-brown as infection progresses.

The spots or lesions are often surrounded by yellow zones


Treatments:-


Bleach treatment : Seed treatment with sodium hypochlorite (for example, Clorox) is effective in reducing bacterial populations on seed surfaces.

Hot water treatment: Seed treatment with hot water is effective in reducing bacterial populations on the surface and inside the seeds.
