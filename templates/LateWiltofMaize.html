Disease Type : Late Wilt of Maize

Symptoms:-



Symptoms are usually observed during or after the flowering stage and vary in intensity depending on the variety of maize and weather conditions

Leaves in the lower part of the plant start to wilt and turn dull Green.

Graduallythe ,they become dry and start to roll inwards,while the symptoms progress upward.

Ears of affected plants do not grow properly, husks may have lesions and grains development is impaired.


Treatments:-


Several Solution based on microorganisms have been tested in laborateries to treat and reduce the incidence of this pathogen

Always consider an intigrated approach with preventive measures together with biological treatments.

As it is a soil and seedborne disease, it is recommended to purchase seeds from a certified source.
