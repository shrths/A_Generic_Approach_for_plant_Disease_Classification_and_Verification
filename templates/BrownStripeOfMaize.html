Plant Disease Detection Sign-Out

Disease Type : Brown Stripe of Maize

Symptoms:-



Early-stage symptoms are visible as flecks or blobs on the lowermost leaves,giving them a burnt appearance.

These yellow stripes will become yellowish-tan to purple and ultimately turn brown

Under high moisture conditions,greyish white downy growth will appear on the lower side of the leaves.


Treatments:-


Always consider an intigrated approach with preventive measures together with biological treatments

Protectant fungicide can help to prevent the contamination of the plant
