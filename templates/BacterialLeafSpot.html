Disease Type : Bacterial Leaf Spot

Symptoms:-



An early symptom of bacterial leaf spot is small (less than 0.25 inch in diameter).

water-soaked leaf spots on the older leaves of the plant. These lesions are typically bordered by leaf veins and angular in shape.

Lesions quickly turn black (a diagnostic characteristic of this disease).


Treatments:-


There are no cures for systemically infected plants and these plants should be discarded.

Individual leaves with spots can be picked off and destroyed.

Any method that will lower the humidity, decrease leaf wetness or increase air circulation will help to lessen the chances of infection.
