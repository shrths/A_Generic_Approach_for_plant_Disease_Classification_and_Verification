Disease Type : Brown Spot Of Rice

Symptoms:-



Brown spot is a fungal disease that infects the coleoptile, leaves, leaf sheath, panicle branches, glumes, and spikelets.

Its most observable damage is the numerous big spots on the leaves which can kill the whole leaf.

When infection occurs in the seed, unfilled grains or spotted or discolored seeds are formed.


Treatments:-


Improving soil fertility is the first step in managing brown spot.

By applying required fertilizers and for soils that are low in silicon, apply calcium silicate slag before planting
